Raspberry tart by Marsin Bakers

Raspberry tart by Marsin Bakers is a tart with a raspberry, flakes of pistachio and a white chocolate cream. Raspberry tart is a marvel, both visually and tastefully. Everything is there, the crunch of a delicious sweet pastry, the delicacy of an almond cream, the smoothness of the mascarpone/raspberry cream, the freshness of the raspberries generously filled with this raspberry compote. This dessert is a real success.
